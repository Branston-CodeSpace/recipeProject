Omega Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp. water
  • Dash salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp. butter
  • 1/3 cup of cheese
  • 1/3 cup of chopped ham

Instructions:

  1. Mix the eggs, water, salt and pepper into a bowl
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan on medium heat
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan
  4. When the mixture has thickened and there is no visible liquid, add filling and fold omelette in half
  5. Serve.
